Hi, I’m Trisha.

I’m a therapist working with adults navigating heavy moments, confused emotions, or difficult bits of life together.

My approach

My practice is trauma-informed, rooted in narrative therapy, and guided by anti-oppressive, anti-caste principles. These aren't just values I hold, they shape how I actually work, through supervision, ongoing training, and lived experience.

I believe the personal is political. The therapy space is not separate from the world around us; our context, identities, and systems all influence how we feel and how we move through life. I try to hold all of that, alongside you.

3+ years of clinical experience · Remote sessions · Age group: 18-45 years · Based in Hyderabad & Mumbai

How I work

Sessions with me are collaborative, paced by you, and open to feedback. Here's what you can expect:

  • Non-pathologising, you are not a diagnosis
  • Aware of how systems around us affect us
  • At your pace, open to feedback
  • Attuned to you and your needs
  • Mindful of social, cultural & political context
  • Building the relationship together

I use an eclectic approach, borrowing from process-based approaches like narrative, mindfulness, and somatic work. I work with DBT, ACT AND IFS.
