A space to slow down, be met, and make sense of things.

Therapy doesn't move in a straight line. It’s like a spiral.

Spiral with each passing round, is a little wider, a little more aware. What once felt heavy becomes something we can sit with, even briefly, before circling on.

Like water finding its way around shore, the spiral holds both return and change at once. Each wave moving differently, never quite in the same shape. The spiral movement offers us to sit with even the uncomfortable bits.

Hi, I'm Trisha.

I use a social justice lens in talk therapy because our struggles don't happen in a vacuum. I work with adults navigating heavy moments, confused emotions, or difficult bits of life. Together we will center your lived experiences, placing the "problems" in its context, and exploring how our bodies respond to them.
